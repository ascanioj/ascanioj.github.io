Scam Detector
“The world's #1 fraud prevention app”

In 2011 Sorin founded Scam Detector. What he didn't know was how popular this app would become.

Scam detector app is a free mobile and web-based application that exposes in detail over 700 of the world's most notorious scams, in all industries. The app educates consumers around the globe how these scams work and teaches them how to prevent financial fraud. Scam detector has been accessed to date in 127 countries and won the 2011 Consumer Champion Awards, hosted annually by the Government of Alberta, Canada.

Every Scam exposed in the app can be instantly shared on Facebook, Twitter, or by e-mail, while users can submit their own scams. Download it now for FREE.

Travel By Dart
Adventure travel meets tv & philantrhopy

Travel by Dart is a new television series that combines adventure travel, reality tv and philanthropy. Each episode two friends throw a dart a the worlds map and travel wherever it lands, with the purpose of heping the people or the land.


The show delivers an inspirin tale of two young men and how they overcome the callenges that face the along the way to complete each mission. Travel to the ends of the earth helping the people and the land, from arctic islands of Svalbard, 300 miles from the north pole addressing the polar bear cause, to a cross-country tour of Russia where they combat youth homelessness and expose mafia violence.


This show is for all ages. Stay tuned at Travelbydart.com as Sorin and Matt continue to film their ongoing journey. They are giving away tons of contest prizes including free trips around the world. Full season is expected to release in summer 2014
